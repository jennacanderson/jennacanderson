# 💫 About Me:
I recently graduated from the University of Iowa with a B.B.A. in Business Analytics and Information Systems and a B.A. in Music. I like writing queries, working through messy data, and building something that makes the answer clear.<br><br>I work primarily in SQL, Python, Excel, Power BI, and Tableau. My focus is relational data modeling, dashboard design, and making sense of datasets that weren't built to be analyzed. I like picking up new tools, and I tend to learn them fastest when I have a real problem to use them on.
